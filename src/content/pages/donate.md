Your generosity sustains NAMCC's daily operations, community services, and the ongoing masjid expansion project. Donations support the food pantry, medical clinic, diaper bank, humanitarian assistance, and general operating expenses that keep our doors open to the community.

Sadaqah, Zakat, and general donations can be made online, by mail, or in person at the masjid office.
