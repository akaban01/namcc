---
title: "Membership"
description: "Become a member of NAMCC and help shape the community's future."
---

Membership at NAMCC gives you a voice in the direction of the community, including participation in board elections and community decisions. Members support the masjid's operations and long-term sustainability through annual dues.

To learn more about membership requirements and to apply, contact the office at info@namcc.org or +1 (512) 491-7148.
