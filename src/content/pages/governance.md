---
title: "Governance"
description: "How NAMCC is governed as a non-profit community organization."
---

NAMCC is organized and operated as a non-profit community organization. Our governance structure includes a Board of Directors, standing committees, and bylaws that guide decision-making, elections, and financial oversight.

We are committed to transparency and accountability to our membership and the broader Central Texas Muslim community. For governing documents, bylaws, or election information, please contact the office at info@namcc.org.
