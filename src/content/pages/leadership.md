NAMCC is led by a dedicated Board of Directors and a team of committee volunteers who oversee the Center's operations, finances, and community programs. Our leadership works together to uphold the vision and mission of the masjid, guided by transparency, consultation (shura), and accountability to the community.

Detailed board member profiles are updated periodically — please check back or contact the office for the current roster.
