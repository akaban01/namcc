---
title: "New Expansion"
description: "NAMCC's project to build a new masjid to serve a growing community."
---

With over 1,000 brothers and sisters now attending Friday prayers, our community has outgrown its current location. NAMCC has initiated a vital project to construct a new masjid on the same premises, ensuring the space can comfortably accommodate our community's needs for years to come, insha Allah.

## Why We're Expanding

- Weekly attendance has grown to over 3,000 worshipers.
- Ramadan nights bring an average of 2,000 daily worshipers for Taraweeh.
- Current facilities are at capacity for prayer space, parking, and community programs.

## How You Can Help

The expansion is community-funded. Donations of all sizes bring us closer to breaking ground on a masjid that will serve generations of Central Texas Muslims.
