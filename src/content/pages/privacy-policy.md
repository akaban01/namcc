NAMCC respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website, forms, and newsletter sign-ups.

## Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you submit a form, subscribe to our newsletter, register for an event, or make a donation.

## How We Use Information

Information is used to communicate with you about events, services, and community updates, and to process donations or volunteer sign-ups. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
