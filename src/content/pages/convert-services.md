NAMCC offers guidance and support for new Muslims (reverts/converts) as they begin their journey in the faith. We aim to make every new Muslim feel welcomed, supported, and connected to the community.

## What We Offer

- One-on-one mentorship with community members and imams.
- Basic Islamic education classes covering prayer, Quran, and daily practice.
- Community integration through events, halaqas, and social gatherings.
- A confidential space to ask questions at any stage of your journey.

To connect with our convert support program, contact info@namcc.org or +1 (512) 491-7148.
