If you're curious about Islam, have questions about faith and practice, or are exploring what it means to be Muslim, our community is here to welcome you.

## Ways to Learn More

- Visit the masjid during any of our five daily prayers or Friday Jumu'ah.
- Speak with one of our imams — office hours are available regularly between Maghrib and Isha.
- Attend a community event or educational class.

## Have Questions?

Reach out to us at info@namcc.org or +1 (512) 491-7148. We're happy to answer questions, share resources, or simply have a conversation.
